The united states was the leader in chip production when morris chang established his factories for chip production in taiwan in the 1980s. He gained american technology from mit and other american research universities. Over decades of government subsidies and easy transfer of american technology morris chang built what is today tsmc. Chang is now looking at the construction of a plant in arizona as a difficult task. Originally from ninbo zhejiang province china and having survived the sino-japanese war and the civil war in china he went to hong kong in 1949.
